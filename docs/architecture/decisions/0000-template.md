# ADR-0000: Template

Copy this file, number it sequentially, and name it after the decision:
`0001-middleware-choice.md`

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by ADR-XXXX
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

---

## Context

What's the situation forcing a decision? What constraints apply — compute budget, cost, your own available time, existing commitments?

Write this before deciding. Stating the problem properly often changes the answer.

## Decision

What you chose. One or two sentences, stated plainly.

## Alternatives considered

What else was on the table, and why each was rejected. **This is the section future-you actually needs.** When you revisit the decision in a year, the question is never "what did I pick" — the code says that. It's "did I already think about X, and what did I conclude?"

## Consequences

What this makes easier. What it makes harder or forecloses. What you're now committed to.

Include the costs honestly. An ADR that only lists benefits is marketing, and it won't help you when the tradeoff comes due.

## Revisit if…

The conditions that would justify reopening this. If none exist, say so.

---

*Records are never deleted. When one is superseded, mark its status and link forward — the reasoning stays useful even after the conclusion changes.*
